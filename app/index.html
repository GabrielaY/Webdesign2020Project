What is TheDogBeastiary?

The dog beastiary is a website where you can get all needed information about your favorite dog breeds while enjoying the rpg vibe from great artwork. We offer you a test to determine which breed of dog is best suitable for your lifestyle. New breeds will be added shortly. For the moment we have over 15 dog breeds avaliable. Thank you for your patience and stay tuned for new updates.

The Dog Beasiary Team

How our test works

The test offered by TheDogBeastiary offers you inovative design and questions. Here we rank different dog breeds on 5 criteria. Energy, temperament, grooming care, training skills and health issues. Click on any of the cards bellow to learn more about each criteria.
