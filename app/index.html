What is TheDogBeastiary?

The dog beastiary is a website where you can get all needed information about your favorite dog breeds while enjoying the rpg vibe from great artwork. We offer you a test to determine which breed of dog is best suitable for your lifestyle. New breeds will be added shortly. For the moment we have over 15 dog breeds avaliable. How our test works

The test offered by TheDogBeastiary offers you inovative design and questions. Here we rank different dog breeds on 5 criteria. Energy, temperament, grooming care, training skills and health issues. Click on any of the cards bellow to learn more about each criteria.

Energy

A dog’s energy level is the single most important factor to consider when choosing a dog. Here’s how to evaluate a low-energy dog, a medium-energy dog, and a high-energy dog.

Grooming

Selective breeding has led to the development of dogs with a number of different coat characteristics and different grooming needs. Some breeds have hair that grows continuously and does not shed.

Temperament

Temperament testing in dogs is most commonly conducted for breeders and rescue organizations, but it can be interesting for any dog owner.

Trainability

A dog’s intelligence is present in a variety of forms, and trainability is a sign of cleverness. Overall, attentiveness, the ability to absorb information quickly, and regular routines maintain a dog’s behavior.

Health

Each dog breed requires speacial care. Get the best health advice from our experts for every stage of a dog's life, from puppy to senior.
